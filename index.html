    OUR PALMS 🌴

    • Washingtonia palm-high for its ornamental value in landscapes and its ability to thrive as a potted plant in its youth.
    • Bismarkia palm-a magnificent,single-trunked,evergreen palm known for its massive,powdery blue-grey fan fronds that can span over 3m across.best used as a stand alone specimen to create a dramatic,tropical,or modern landscape design.
    • Traveller palm-the plant is named for its ability to store rainwater in leaf sheathes(bases).ideal as standalone centerpiece in gardens,near swimming pools,or at the entrance of homes to create a "grand" or "welcoming" tropical statement.
    • Pencil palm-they promote sustainability,biodegradeable .Popular fo promotions,corporate gifts,and sustainable stationery.
    • Silver Pine-common ornamental evergreen plant featuring soft,silvery-blue foliage and a pyramid shape,often for small to medium sized gardens.
    • Rubra Palm-it has high ornamental value .used for landscaping. planted in gardens due to its elegant,arching fronds and red pigmentation.
    • Foxtail Palm-ornamental plant valued for its unique,bushy fronds that resembles a fox's tail and its self cleaning trunk.used in landscaping of both residential and commercial projects.
    • Fishtail Palm-used as specimen plant,a focal point in tropical designs,or planted in rows as a dense privacy screen or hedge.known to improve air quality.In traditional practice some parts are useed in remedies.
    • Phoenix Palm-known for its majestic appearance.Excellent for landscaping in Parks,gardens,and along hotel boulevards to provide a tropical oasis feel.
    • Other various plants/trees are available like:Cyprus,Terminalia tree,Mango tree,Malta tree,Fiscus Macrocarpa etc.
